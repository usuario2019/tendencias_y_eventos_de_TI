Presentation

According to the business evolution it has been mandatory for the companies to improve its structure, that is the reason why the Informatic Technologies (TI) Department is part of the main bases of the new development companies’ scheme. The TI associated to the Enterprise Informational System (SIE) allow to keep the orientation and competitivity in the market by the firm. Global Events as the new actualization of the DNS servers, The DNS Flag Day, which take place the next 1st of February of 2019 could affect the information systems available, this is motive to have the human resource to verify compatibilities, make the integrations and support the system in real time to decrease and solve the failures and to enhance the development of the business. The implementation of Bind, Unbound, PowerDNS and Knot (Fig. 01) the principal DNS software suppliers, is the reality which face the companies in order to solve the conducts out of normal in a standard of a DNS authoritative. In the TI ambit’s the global events make the opportunity to success or the risk to a big failure, TI´s good practices are focused in the objectives not only individual but also the group goals which it´s guarantee the good results. In this case the purpose is the technological linking up without the collateral effects which break the normal develop of the activities. This document describes good practices of TI for an event as the one mentioned before.
